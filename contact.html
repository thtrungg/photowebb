Chinh is a Ha Noi based cinematographer and colorist originally from Washington, DC.

He shoots a diverse range of material, from narrative films to music videos, always tailoring his use of lighting, composition, color, and camera movement to the needs of each project.

The imaging pipeline is very important to Chinh. He enjoys being part of the entire process from acquisition, to preparation, to display. Beyond on-set production, he is passionate about image processing, color grading, and film emulation.

Chinh’s recent DP work includes shooting a WWII narrative project starring David Mazouz and Aoi Takeya, and shooting music videos for Bennett Coast, Chris Emond, and Benji Patterson.

As a colorist, Chinh has worked with a variety of brands and artists including Pokémon, Vince Staples, Swae Lee, Mabel, and others.
